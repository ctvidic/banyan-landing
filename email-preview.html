Email Deliverability Tips

Why This Design Helps:

  1. Personal tone: Emails from real people go to primary inbox
  2. No promotional language: Avoids spam triggers
  3. Clean design: Less HTML = better deliverability
  4. Reply encouragement: Engagement signals improve reputation
  5. Clear unsubscribe: Shows legitimacy to email providers
